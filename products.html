About Aoura Products

Aoura Products operates as the central trading and distribution arm of Aoura Group. We provide seamless wholesale supply to businesses, streamlining corporate procurement.

  • Office Supplies: Comprehensive stationery, coffee, cleaning, and essential corporate supplies.
  • Industrial Products: High-precision manufacturing and industrial equipment including laser protective lenses.
  • Packaging & Shipping: Logistics supplies such as bubble wraps and advanced packaging materials.
  • Industrial Safety: Premium safety gear including Redwing Safety Footwear.

With head offices in Dubai and warehouses located in Deira (Dubai) and Sajjah Industrial (Sharjah), Aoura Products ensures fast delivery networks and robust B2B support.

A UAE-based holding company managing supply chains and e-commerce services to power regional commerce.
